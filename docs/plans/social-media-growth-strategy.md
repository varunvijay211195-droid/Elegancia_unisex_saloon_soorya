## 💡 Strategy 1: Automated Review Generation System

### How It Works:
After every service, your admin panel triggers:
1. **SMS/WhatsApp follow-up** asking for feedback
2. **Easy review links** to Google, Facebook, Instagram
3. **Incentives** for leaving reviews (discount on next visit)

### Admin Panel Features:
- Review request templates
- Auto-send schedule (1 day post-service)
- Track review status (pending/completed)
- Monitor average ratings

### Impact:
- ⬆️ Google reviews → ⬆️ Local SEO → ⬆️ New customers
- Social proof increases conversion by 270%
